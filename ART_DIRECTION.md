# Art Direction

## Style Target

Japanese cel-animation clarity with painterly backgrounds: clear dark contour lines, low-saturation warm colors, cel-shaded forms, visible thick-brush texture in the environment, and cozy desk-window lighting.

## Composition Rule

Only the homepage hero may show full dog and cat characters. All later website visuals should shift to objects, rooms, tools, food, notebooks, bowls, shelves, windows, and quiet editorial still-life scenes. Avoid full pet bodies, pet portraits, pet faces, tails, noses, or character-style animal cameos outside the hero.
